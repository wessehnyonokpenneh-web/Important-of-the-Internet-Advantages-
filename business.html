E-Commerce Revolution

The internet has revolutionized commerce. Businesses no longer depend only on physical stores. Online shopping platforms allow customers to buy products from anywhere in the world.

Digital Advertising

Companies use social media, websites, and search engines to advertise their products to millions of users.

Online Payments

Internet banking and mobile money systems allow secure and fast transactions without physical cash.

Employment Opportunities

The internet has created jobs like freelancing, content creation, software development, and online teaching.

Many people now earn income from home using internet-based platforms.
