Instant Communication

The internet allows people to communicate instantly regardless of distance. Messages can be sent in seconds using email, social media, and messaging apps.

Video Communication

Video calls allow people to communicate face-to-face in real time. This is important for business meetings, online learning, and family connections.

Social Networking

Social media has changed the way people interact. It allows sharing of ideas, news, photos, and videos with a large audience.

Global Connectivity

The internet connects the world into a global village where people from different countries can interact easily.
